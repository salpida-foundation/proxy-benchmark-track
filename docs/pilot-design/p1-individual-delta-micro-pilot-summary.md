# P1 — Individual Delta Micro Pilot (Public Summary)

> ## ⚠️ SCOPE BOUNDARY
> This is a **public design-reference summary only**. It is not an IRB document,
> not a preregistration, and not a consent form. It contains **no raw human data,
> no participant data, no consent text, and no real stimuli**. Human-subject
> materials live in a private IRB / OSF workspace, not in this public repository.
> Source of record: `docs/pilot-design/proxy-benchmark-pilot-design-v1.1.md` (section 4).

## Goal

Confirm that an individual's ΔState before and after an AI response is **measurable**.

## Design

- Within-subject, **2 conditions: A (Validating-regulatory) vs D (Minimizing-dismissive)**.
- n = 20–30.
- IRB required; applied for expedited review as non-clinical, minimal risk.
- Low-intensity everyday scenarios only. Excludes trauma, self-harm, domestic
  violence, and medical / legal / financial / crisis content.

## Measurement (summary)

- Momentary affect: SAM (9-point pictorial); license/figure-use/translation to be
  confirmed before any human-subject deployment.
- State clarity, emotional stability, relational trust, distress: single-item scales.
- STAI is **not** used (Mind Garden license).
- Timepoints: T0 baseline → T1 immediately after AI response → T2 (5 min later).
- ΔState = T1 − T0; Persistence = T2 − T1.

## Pass criteria

A vs D directionality consistent · missing rate < 10% · distress threshold
exceeded in < 5% of participants · no adverse events.

## Safety

Distress measured 1–7 after each condition; **≥ 5 → pause/terminate + connect a
coordinator**. Participants may stop at any time.

## Not included here

Raw data, consent details, full stimuli, and the IRB submission package are
intentionally **out of scope for this public repository**.
